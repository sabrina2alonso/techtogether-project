Activites


What are you supposed to do now that you're here in Miami? Is there any activities that are accessible to me? We provide you with a list of places to go and activites to do during your stay.



Our top picks:


New World Symphony:

  • Provides a sensory-friendly environment for anyone who may have austism and/or sensory processing dirorders
  • Live stream concert feed with reduced volume, noise-redacting headphones, and muted lighting
  • Also a quiet room availible

The beach:

  • Free manual or motorized wheelchairs
  • wheelchair access to the beach via Mobi-Mats

Shake-A-Leg:

  • Works with adults and kids with physical, developmental, and econimic challenges
  • Array of theraputic/ relaxing watersports

Miami Zoos:

  • Plans on creating sensory bags including noise-cancelling headphones and fidget toys
  • Quiet spaces
  • Monorails and trams for wheelchair accessibility
