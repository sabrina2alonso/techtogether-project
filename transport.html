Transportation


You're in a new city with no way of geting around. Luckiily, Miami Dade County provides a multitude of accessible transportation, inclusing busses, taxis, and the metrorail.



What do these services provide?


Metrorail:

  • Stations for wheelchair/ mobility aids
  • Announcment system alerts if the elevators are out of order and where
  • Backup service within 30 minutes of out of order elevator service*
  • Free parking for riders with disabilities

Taxicabs:

  • Wheelchair boarding / accessibility

Metrobus:

  • Wheelchairs/ mobility aids have priority boarding
  • Stop announcments are announced

General Metromover Services:

  • Special needs emergency evacuation process set in place
  • Service animals allowed
